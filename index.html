Ana Garcia del Molino

I am a PhD candidate at the School of Computer Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, and the Institute for Infocomm Research (A*STAR), under the Singapore International Graduate Award (SINGA) program. My supervisors are Lim Joo-Hwee and Tan Ah-Hwee. My research interests are in the areas of computer vision, pattern recognition and cognitive vision. In particular, my PhD topic is the summarization of egocentric and consumer videos.

I obtained my BSc and MSc in Telecommunications Engineering from UPC BarcelonaTech, where I collaborated with GPI. My MSc thesis was co-supervised by Shin'ichi Satoh at the NII of Japan.
